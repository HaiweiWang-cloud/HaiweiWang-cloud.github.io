Tidbits

26/11/2023

I built a simple audio amplifier on a breadboard with a single stage common-emitter amplifier! I can't believe it worked first time! Now I finally have a speaker for my PC.

This design is not the best. For one, it draws power even with no audio input, the sound quality is acceptable but quite noisy, and it could do with more volume and maybe a volume control knob.

This is only the most basic type of amplifier, and knowing that it works, I will make continuous incremental improvements to get a sick home audio system. This project will be documented in a post coming soon!
